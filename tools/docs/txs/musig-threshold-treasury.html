How Musig_Threshold Treasury Works
1.
The MUSIG_THRESHOLD block stores three fields: agg_pubkey (the aggregate public key), threshold, and group_size.
2.
Unlike MULTISIG which reveals M individual keys + signatures, MuSig produces a single aggregate signature. On-chain cost is constant regardless of group size.
3.
The evaluator verifies the single signature against the aggregate key. The threshold/group_size fields are informational — the cryptographic aggregation happens off-chain.
4.
This is ideal for DAO treasuries and large organizations where revealing individual signers is undesirable and on-chain costs must be minimized.
